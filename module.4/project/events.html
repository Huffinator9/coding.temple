Events and Activities

Check out our assortment of activities and regular events. You're sure to find something for each member of the family.

Arcade Cabinets

It is our goal to have a wide variety of arcade cabinets. From the classics to modern favorites.

  • A blast from the past comes in the form of Donkey Kong, Dig Dug, and Pac-Man.
  • Test your hunting skills with Big Game Hunter Reloaded.
  • Try your moves on the dance floor with DDR.
  • We also have Air Hockey, a Ping Pong table, and Pool Tables.

Laser Tag

With one of the only facilities with moveable partitions, our laser tag arena is fully modular and we rotate the layout monthly. We are currently running a castle dungeon theme and layout. Stop by next month for our Enchanted Forest Theme or book a party and choose from five different themes, each with their own layout. Contact Us for more information.

Magic: The Gathering

Stop by and bring your deck to play against other locals in the industry leading Trading Card Game from Wizards of the Coast. Don't have a deck of your own or are just starting? Don't worry. We offer starter decks so you can get your feet wet and see if Magic: The Gathering is right for you. Then purchase your own cards and build a deck or purchase one of our pre-builts.

Nintendo Switch

Come play the newest Nintendo games and classics alike in your very own room. Where you and your friends can have privacy and show off your skills.

Drop in for our Weeklies

  • Humpday is the day for Smash. Brawl it out in our weekly bracket.
  • End the work week with Magic: The Gathering Drafts. Can you build the best deck on the fly?

Monthly Laser Tag Tournament

  • The last Sat. is reserved for the Laser Tag Tournament. Battle against friend and foe for the grand prize, a laser tag punch card, good for 10 free games.

Other Offerings

  • Head over to the Contact page to book your personal event.
  • We have many bundles including Laser Tag punch cards and vouchers for games.
  • We have many charity drives that we put on throughout the year. Currently, we are promoting one for my daughter who is in the final stages of Leukemia Therapy.
